**Cardboard Castle Club** is a community-driven children's theatre group for kids ages 6–16. We produce original plays, musicals, and variety shows — all written, designed, and performed by our incredible young members. And yes, our sets are mostly made of cardboard.

We believe that theatre isn't just about what happens on stage. It's about what happens *inside* a kid when they realize they can be brave, be loud, be silly, be someone else for a while — and then come back to themselves a little more confident than before.

Our programs are inclusive, affordable, and hands-on. Whether your kid is a born performer or someone who'd rather build the sets and stay behind the curtain, we'll find their spark.
