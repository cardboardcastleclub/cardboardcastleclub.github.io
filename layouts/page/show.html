About the Show

When a lonely kid named Kit discovers a doorway hidden inside a cardboard box, they tumble into the Kingdom of Nowhere — a world built entirely from things people threw away. There they meet a knight made of tin foil, a queen who rules from a refrigerator-box throne, and a dragon made of bubble wrap who's more scared of them than they are of it.

The Kingdom of Nowhere is an original musical written and designed collaboratively by the kids of Cardboard Castle Club. It's a story about finding treasure in what others see as trash, and discovering that the most extraordinary worlds are the ones you build yourself.
