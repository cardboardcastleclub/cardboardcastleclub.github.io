About Us

We're a bunch of kids (and a few grown-ups) who believe you can build anything out of cardboard and imagination.

Cardboard Castle Club is a community-driven children’s theatre group for kids ages 6–16. We produce original plays, musicals, and variety shows — all written, designed, and performed by our incredible young members. And yes, our sets are mostly made of cardboard.

We believe that theatre isn’t just about what happens on stage. It’s about what happens inside a kid when they realize they can be brave, be loud, be silly, be someone else for a while — and then come back to themselves a little more confident than before.

Our programs are inclusive, affordable, and hands-on. Whether your kid is a born performer or someone who’d rather build the sets and stay behind the curtain, we’ll find their spark.

What We Believe

01

Every Child Is Creative

We believe that every young person has something extraordinary inside them. Our job is to hand them a paintbrush, a script, and a stage made of boxes.

02

Process Over Perfection

The magic isn't just in the final bow — it's in every rehearsal, every wobbly set piece, every "let's try that again." We celebrate the mess.

03

Inclusion Is Non-Negotiable

No auditions to "get in." No one sits on the sidelines. Every child who walks through our doors gets a role, a voice, and a place to belong.

04

DIY Is the Way

We don't need fancy equipment. A refrigerator box becomes a spaceship. A bedsheet becomes a waterfall. Imagination is our biggest budget line.

Our Story

2019

The First Box

Founded by a group of parents who wanted their kids to have a creative outlet beyond screens. Started with 8 kids, a garage, and a stack of Amazon boxes.

2020

Going Virtual

When the world shut down, we went online. Kids performed monologues from their bedrooms and built mini sets out of shoeboxes. The show went on.

2022

Our First Big Show

We staged our first full-length production — a kid-written original called "The Kingdom of Nowhere." The entire set was built from recycled cardboard. Standing ovation.

2024

Building the Castle

Now over 40 active members, multiple productions a year, and a permanent rehearsal space (with a truly impressive cardboard castle in the corner).

Now

The Next Chapter

We're bigger than ever and working on our most ambitious build yet. Come be part of the story.

Ready to Build With Us?

New members are always welcome. No experience needed — just bring your imagination (and maybe an empty box)!
