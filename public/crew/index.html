The Crew

Meet the talented humans who build the castle and bring the magic.

Every production is a team effort. From the directors who guide the vision to the kids who bring it to life — on stage and behind the scenes — here is the incredible crew behind The Kingdom of Nowhere.

🎬 Directors & Staff

🎬

Maria Santos

Artistic Director & Founder

Former Broadway stage manager turned community theatre evangelist. Maria founded the club in 2019 with nothing but eight kids and a pile of moving boxes.

🎵

Devon Park

Music Director

Classically trained pianist who believes every kid can carry a tune — they just need someone to believe in them first.

💃

Aisha Thompson

Choreographer

Dance teacher by day, choreographer by weekend. Her motto: 'If you can walk, you can dance. If you can talk, you can sing.'

🔨

Bill Kowalski

Master Builder

Retired carpenter who builds our cardboard sets with the kids. His philosophy: 'A wobbly castle built by a proud kid beats a perfect one every time.'
