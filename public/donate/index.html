Support Us

Help us keep building castles and raising curtains for every kid who wants to create.

Cardboard Castle Club is a nonprofit, volunteer-run organization. We keep our fees as low as possible so that no child is ever turned away. But costumes, supplies, lighting, and venue rentals add up fast — even when your sets are made of free boxes.

Your donation — no matter the size — helps us continue to provide a creative home for kids in our community. Every dollar goes directly to supporting our productions and programs.
